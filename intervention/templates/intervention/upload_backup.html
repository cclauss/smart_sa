Instructions for restoral

  1. Save the appropriate download here as restore.html
  2. Copy it to the laptop, putting it in your "My Documents" folder. NOTE: You may have to overwrite the current file there
  3. Do NOT open the file directly
  4. Open Masivukeni as usual.
  5. Do NOT log in immediately. Click 'Go to restore.html in My Documents' link
    6. This will open the restore file. It is necessary to open this file once before going through the rest of the restore procedure.
  6. Click the link to 'Administrator Log In'
  7. Log in with your password
  8. Look for Restore From Backup and click the button that says 'show available backups'. You should see a link that includes today's date. Click this link. The restore process will now begin. This may take up to two minutes. Do not reload or leave the page until a message appears that says the data is restored.
